About Dwell Ministries

Dwell Ministries Inc. is a Christ-centered, fully virtual ministry where God dwells and transformation happens. We exist to reach hearts, restore families, and raise up disciples through prayer, worship, and the Word. Everything online and it’s all for God’s glory.

“The Lord has done great things for us, and we are filled with joy.” – Psalm 126:3

Amid the global pandemic in 2020, God began a remarkable work. What started as a simple family Zoom prayer and praise session quickly became a consistent gathering for Sunday services, family check-ins, and spiritual encouragement. Over the years, as some people moved on and new ones joined, the Holy Spirit guided us. We didn’t understand the journey at the start. However, the more we prayed and were obedient, the more the call to pastoral ministry became evident.

Pastor Wallace & Erin Ford
