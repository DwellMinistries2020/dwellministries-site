About the Pastor

Pastor Wallace Ford is a passionate leader who carries the heart of God and the desire to see people healed, restored, and walking in their divine purpose. With years of experience ministering to youth, families, and communities, he is committed to equipping disciples through sound doctrine, worship, and the power of prayer. As the founder and lead pastor of Dwell Ministries Inc., he believes in creating a space where people can truly dwell with God, online and in their everyday lives.
